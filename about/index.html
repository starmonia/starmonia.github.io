About The Adventures of Starmonia

Welcome to The Adventures of Starmonia

This is a collection of enchanting stories centered around Starmonia—a character inspired by the mysterious gaze of eyes (light blue and green) and the spirit of a husky (black and white).

About This Site

This storybook is a simple, easy-to-manage platform for sharing multi-book series with chapters. Whether you’re a writer, storyteller, or dreamer, this site makes it effortless to publish and organize your tales.

The Color Palette

  • Light Blue & Green: Representing the curious, intelligent eyes that perceive worlds beyond
  • Black & White: Honoring the loyal, spirited nature of a husky companion

How It Works

This site is built with Jekyll, a simple static site generator perfect for GitHub Pages. All stories are managed through a single YAML file—no complex coding required.

Getting Started

To add new books and chapters, see the Storybook Management Guide.
