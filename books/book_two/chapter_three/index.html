Chapter 3

The Bridge Keeper

The bridge was magnificent, spanning a vast chasm. Its railings glowed with ethereal light, and in the center stood a figure—the Bridge Keeper, an ancient guardian of the crossing.

“Many have sought passage,” the Keeper said, eyes like starlight. “But few have the courage to face what lies on the other side. Will you?”
